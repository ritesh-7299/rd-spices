# Business Plan Kit

This kit now contains a full execution package for your spice export business.

## Core Strategy Files
1. `business_plan_master_2026_2027.md`
- Founder-level strategy: which countries, which products, how to execute, how to control risk.

2. `country_priority_matrix.csv`
- Ranked country list with tier, entry difficulty, and year-1 account targets.

3. `product_portfolio_scorecard.csv`
- Phase 1 and Phase 2 product decisions with margin/risk/spec guidance.

4. `compliance_market_checklist.csv`
- Market-wise compliance gates for India, USA, EU/UK, GCC, Canada, Australia.

5. `risk_register_top10.csv`
- Top operational and compliance risks with mitigation and KPI tracking.

6. `execution_12m_operating_plan.csv`
- Month-wise pipeline and conversion targets.

7. `website_mobile_first_blueprint.md`
- B2B conversion-focused website strategy, with strong mobile guidance.

## Financial + Sales Execution Files
8. `financial_model_12m_template.csv`
- Monthly model (Jul 2026 to Jun 2027) with funnel, revenue, margin, break-even, CCC.

9. `financial_model_scenarios.csv`
- Downside/Base/Upside assumptions and outputs.

10. `target_account_list_200_template.csv`
- 200 pre-numbered account rows (`A001` to `A200`) for pipeline building.

11. `outbound_kit.md`
- Cold email, LinkedIn scripts, qualification call script, and sample-to-PO playbook.

## Recommended Usage Order
1. Read `business_plan_master_2026_2027.md` and lock Year-1 scope.
2. Fill `country_priority_matrix.csv` and `product_portfolio_scorecard.csv` for your final choices.
3. Start outreach using `target_account_list_200_template.csv` + `outbound_kit.md`.
4. Track targets in `execution_12m_operating_plan.csv` every week.
5. Keep `financial_model_12m_template.csv` updated monthly.

## Important Note
This pack is designed for practical execution. Before any shipment, validate market-specific legal requirements with your customs broker and destination importer.
