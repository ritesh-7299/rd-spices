# Mobile-First Website Blueprint (B2B Spice Export)

## Purpose
Build a website that makes importers trust you fast and sends qualified RFQs.

## Core Positioning
- Premium Indian spice exporter
- Compliance-driven and shipment-reliable
- Built for long-term importer relationships

## Page Architecture
1. Home
2. About
3. Products
4. Export Capabilities
5. Quality Control
6. Certifications
7. Contact / RFQ

## Homepage Flow (Mobile)
1. Hero: clear export promise + one primary CTA
2. Proof bar: certifications, countries served, years of experience
3. Featured products: 6-8 cards with quick specs
4. Why choose us: consistency, testing, documentation, logistics
5. Process: sourcing to shipment timeline
6. Industries served: importer, manufacturer, private label
7. FAQ: MOQ, lead time, quality docs, payment terms
8. Final CTA: Request quote and WhatsApp contact

## Mobile UX Rules
- Use short headline blocks and compact cards
- Keep sticky CTA button visible (quote or WhatsApp)
- Keep forms short and easy to finish on phone
- Optimize images for fast load and clear product identity
- Use simple typography and strong contrast

## Conversion Elements
- RFQ form with essential fields only
- Downloadable product and specification sheet
- Response SLA statement (for example: quote within 24 business hours)
- Clear trust proof: lab testing, certifications, shipment documents

## SEO Basics
- Build one target keyword cluster per page
- Add country-specific and product-specific landing content
- Use internal links between products, quality, and contact pages
- Add schema for organization and products

## Performance Targets
- First contentful paint under 2.5 seconds on mobile
- RFQ form completion rate above 3-5% of qualified traffic
- Bounce rate improvement month over month

Use this blueprint with the market and product priorities in the business plan so your website and sales team are aligned.
