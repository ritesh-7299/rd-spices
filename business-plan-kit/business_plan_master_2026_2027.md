Why Phase 1 works:

### Phase 2 (After Process Stability)

### Defer in Year 1

- Bay leaf: lower differentiation and smaller structured demand signals

## 3) How to Win (Operating Model)

### Buyer Segments
1. Importers/Distributors: fastest deal cycle, bulk movement
2. Food Manufacturers: higher retention, stricter quality requirements
3. Private Label Retail: highest margin, longest onboarding cycle

### Offer Architecture
- Three quality tiers for each core SKU:
  - Economy
  - Standard
  - Premium (sterilized, tighter specs)
- Formats:
  - Whole bulk
  - Ground bulk
  - Custom blend
  - Retail/private label pack

### Commercial Policy
- Incoterms:
  - Sea shipments: FOB/CIF
  - Mixed or multimodal flows: FCA/CIP style logic
- Quote formula:
  - FOB = product cost + inland + port/CHA + testing/docs + finance buffer + margin
  - CIF = FOB + freight + insurance
- Payment ladder:
  1. New buyer: advance for trial PO
  2. Early trust: partial advance + balance on shipping docs
  3. Proven buyer: LC at sight or CAD
  4. Strategic repeat buyer: short credit only with strict limits

### Sample-to-PO Motion
1. Requirement intake
2. Internal feasibility
3. Quote + spec pack
4. Sample dispatch + test docs
5. Technical feedback closure
6. Trial PO
7. First shipment
8. Post-shipment review and reorder calendar

## 5) Compliance and Quality System (Non-Negotiable)

### India-side Base Stack
- IEC
- Spices Board CRES / relevant RCMC
- FSSAI license
- GST LUT or IGST route setup
- Lab tie-ups and documented FSMS/GMP/HACCP process controls

### Market-side Control Points
- USA: FDA facility + importer FSVP readiness + prior notice discipline
- EU/UK: residue, contaminants, and labeling control pack before dispatch
- GCC: product registration and Arabic-ready label workflow
- Canada: SFC importer compliance checks
- Australia: BICON/IFIS alignment before shipment

### Pre-shipment Gate (Operational SOP)
1. Market-specific spec freeze
2. Lab testing batch release
3. Document pack creation
4. Import filing confirmations
5. Final cross-check: HS code, quantity, buyer details, labels
6. Container and pallet check
7. Dispatch approval sign-off
