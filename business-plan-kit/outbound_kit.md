# Outbound Kit (Spice Export)

## 1) ICP + Offer Mapping

1. Importer/Distributor
- Best offer: bulk whole spices + stable monthly dispatch
- Message angle: consistency, clean docs, predictable lead times

2. Food Manufacturer / Seasoning Producer
- Best offer: spec-based whole + ground (micro/residue controlled)
- Message angle: QA reliability, batch consistency, technical compliance

3. Private Label Brand / Retail
- Best offer: finished packs + label support + compliance-ready paperwork
- Message angle: speed to shelf, quality + traceability story

## 4) Qualification Call Script (20-30 min)

### Section A: Requirement Fit
1. Which products are active in your buying program right now?
2. What monthly/quarterly volume do you target per SKU?
3. Which origins are you currently using?

### Section B: Technical + QA
1. Which specifications are non-negotiable (purity, moisture, SHU, curcumin, density)?
2. What testing/COA format do you need pre-dispatch?
3. Any market-specific compliance flags we should plan for?

### Section C: Commercial + Logistics
1. Preferred Incoterm (FOB/CIF/CFR)?
2. Preferred load plan and packaging type?
3. Target lead time and delivery window?

### Section D: Commercial Close
1. Who are decision makers in this purchase process?
2. What timeline do you have for next PO?
3. What is the best next step: sample, spec alignment, or quote?

## 5) Sample-to-PO Playbook

### Step 1: RFQ Intake (Day 0-1)
1. Capture product, grade, quantity, destination, Incoterm.
2. Confirm technical parameters and documentation expectations.

### Step 2: Internal Feasibility (Day 1-2)
1. Verify sourcing and processing feasibility.
2. Confirm expected lead time and lot readiness window.

### Step 3: Quote + Spec Pack (Day 2-3)
1. Send structured quote.
2. Attach spec sheet, packing options, compliance summary.

### Step 4: Sample Cycle (Day 4-12)
1. Dispatch sample with reference specification.
2. Track technical feedback and revisions.

### Step 5: Commercial Confirmation (Day 12-18)
1. Finalize Incoterm, price, payment, dispatch schedule.
2. Freeze packing, labels, and required documents.

### Step 6: PO to Dispatch
1. Execute pre-shipment QA and documentation checklist.
2. Share milestone updates: production complete, stuffing, vessel ETD/ETA.

### Step 7: Post-Shipment Repeat Motion
1. Conduct post-delivery review.
2. Lock repeat-order cadence and forecast.
